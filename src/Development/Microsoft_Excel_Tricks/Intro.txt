> How to combine 3 or more csv/excel files, into one Excel Sheet?
   . Copy the location/path of all the csv files
   . Go to Excel Sheet --> Get Data --> From File --> From Folder
   . Choose "Combine and Load" and hit "Ok" button

> How to copy content of an excel sheet and paste it?
   . Select the contents
   . Hold "Ctrl" and when "+" pops up drag the content to another cell
