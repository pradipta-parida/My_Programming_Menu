> A Data can be classified into Bigdata under 5V's;
    Volume , Velocity , Variety, Veracity, Value

> Example: Considring a Healthcare Industry

        Volume- Hospital,clinics around the world generate massive amounts of data(2314 Exabytes) anually.
        Velocity- In the form of patient records, test results. All these data are generated at a very high speed.
        Variety- Data in the form of structured data(excel records), semi-structured(log files), unstructured data(X-ray images).
        Veracity- Accuracy, Trustworthiness
        Value- Faster disease detection, Better Treatment, Reduced Costs
